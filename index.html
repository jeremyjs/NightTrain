Have you always wanted to learn?

Purdue Night Train is a social swing dance club that promotes fun and community. Here at Night Train we learn all types of swing dance, including East Coast, Lindy Hop, Blues, Charleston, and Balboa (to name a few). We have weekly lessons and social dances every Wednesday night. Our Wednesday Night Jams are at 9:30 and are usually located in the South Tower at the Purdue Memorial Union (this is subject to change.) Night Train is a Purdue University organization that welcomes students, faculty, and even members of the community! To learn more about lessons click here!
