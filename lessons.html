Fall Lessons

Lessons during the school semesters are $25. This fee includes membership and access to all of the dances in addition to weekly lessons. Lessons begin on September 17. Check back soon for more information!

Intro to Swing (Level 1)

If you're an absolute beginner or just want to review the basics, these lessons are for you! The level one lessons will introduce you to two different styles of swing dance, East Coast and Lindy Hop. In addition, you will learn tons of different moves to spice up your dances. Lessons are offered twice a week but the same material is covered in both lessons. Pick one that works for you or come to both for extra practice!

9:30 - 11:30pm every Tuesday in STEW 306
7:00 - 9:00pm every Thursday in MTHW
- Check the calendar on the Home Page for details!

Fundamentals of Lindy (Level 2)

Learn from the best and get personalized coaching in Lindy Hop! If you've already got the basics down and are interested in working on your technique, check out these level 2 lessons. We request that only those who have attended a semester of Level 1 lessons (or already have a background in swing dancing) attend Level 2.

7:00 - 9:00pm every Tuesday in ME 1051
- Check the calendar on the Home Page for details!

Open Track (All Levels) - New!

Open track is a brand new class this semester open to all levels of dancers. Interesting yet simple moves and techniques will be taught at each class. In addition, in the first half of the semester, we will introduce the history of swing dance, music, and culture to welcome people into the swing dance community. The second half of the semester will involve more dancing, focusing on how to make dancing fun, as well as improving your skills.

Instructors: Yulai Liu & Guest Instructors
7:00 - 8:00pm every Wednesday in STEW B2 (Night Train Office)
- Check the calendar on the Home Page for details!
