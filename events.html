Night Train has holds three major events each year: Whistle Stop, Blues in the Nite, and LAFLX. Throughout the year, we also have smaller events such as our Welcome Back Dance and Winter Formal. Check out our facebook page to find out when our next event is.

Welcome Back Dance

Date: September 7th

The Welcome Back Dance is the perfect chance for you to kickstart your semester of swing. Check back for more information as we get closer to the event.

Whistle Stop

More Info! Register Now!
Date: November 15-17.

Whistle Stop is Purdue Night Train's annual fall workshop. Every year, wonderful instructors from around the nation are brought in to teach Lindy Hop, Charleston and/or Balboa.

Blues in the Night

Date: January 17-20.

Blues in the Nite is the longest running blues workshop in Indiana, and has become one of the most highly anticipated events in the Midwest selling out in just a few weeks each year. With 5 hours of lessons from national instructors and 9 hours of dancing, dancers are sure to come away wanting more.

LAFLX

Date: April 4-6.

As the oldest exchange in Indiana, LAFLX is Purdue Night Train's annual exchange located in West Lafayette, IN. Expect over 20 hours of dancing with 3 live bands and a number of DJs ready to provide great music. In addition to dancing and live music, LAFLX also has a free cookout on Saturday.
