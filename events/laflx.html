BANDS

THE BIG SWING BAND

The Big Swing Band is a 19 piece big band that is gaining popularity in Indiana with its sound of classic swing music which captivated the nation from the 1930's through the 1960's. The players are professionals and highly skilled performers from various backgrounds. They play mostly standards from yesteryear recorded by such legends as Glenn Miller, Count Basie, Frank Sinatra, Bobby Darin and Nat King Cole, with some more modern favorites by such artists as Billy Joel, Brian Setzer and the Cherry Poppin' Daddies. The band also sports its own arsenal of original arrangements as well as a few original tunes.



THE FAT BABIES

The Fat Babies are a seven piece jazz band interpreting classic styles of the 1920s and 30s. Founded in 2010 by string bass player Beau Sample, its members include Andy Schumm (cornet), John Otto(reeds), Dave Bock (trombone), Jake Sanders (banjo and guitar), Paul Asaro (piano), and Alex Hall (drums).





COREY DENNISON

Corey has been playing rhythm guitar for Carl Weathersby sharing the stage with some of NorthWest Indiana and Chicagos Monsters of the Midway The Kinsey Report, Robert Blaine, Chico Banks, Theo Huff, The SteepWater Band,Jimmy Johnson, Gerry Hundt, Lurrie bell, Nick Moss, Carlos Johnson, Bill "Buddah" Dickens and The One and only Buddy guy. Corey has also been Blessed to have shared the stage with some of todays National acts Robert Randolph, Derek Trucks And John Mayer.
